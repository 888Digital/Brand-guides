MIAMILOTS

Junior Designer Quick Guide

Everything you need to create high-performing banners and emails that drive B2B sales. Follow these rules, use the templates, and your work will convert.

🎯 Your Mission as a Miamilots Designer

You are NOT creating "pretty graphics." You are creating sales tools for professional resellers who need to make fast business decisions. Every banner you create must answer three questions in 3 seconds:

1. What brand is this?
2. What's the price or discount?
3. How many units are available?

If your design doesn't answer these instantly, start over.

1 Quick Start: Choose Your Template

Don't start from scratch. Pick the template that matches your goal, then customize with your product data.

📦

Template A: New Arrival

USE WHEN: Fresh inventory just arrived
Hierarchy:
1. Brand Logo/Name (largest)
2. Quantity Available
3. Price per unit
4. "NEW" badge
5. CTA: "View Inventory"
🔥

Template B: Flash Sale

USE WHEN: Need to clear inventory FAST
Hierarchy:
1. Discount % (HUGE)
2. Original vs Sale Price
3. Brand Name
4. Deadline (Ends Monday)
5. CTA: "Shop Now"
📂

Template C: Category Promo

USE WHEN: Promoting entire category
Hierarchy:
1. Category Name + Icon
2. "Starting at $X"
3. Total units available
4. Top brands included
5. CTA: "Browse Category"

⚠️ Golden Rule:

If a "Flexible" element (campaign name) is bigger or bolder than a "Critical" element (price/brand), you've failed. Start over.
